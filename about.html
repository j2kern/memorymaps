Welcome to Memorymaps.

"People with dementia, of which Alzheimer’s disease is the most common
cause in seniors, have cognitive impairments that prevent them from
recognizing familiar signposts in their environment, causing them to
easily become lost...”

Here at Memorymaps, we focus on ensuring all patients who suffer with
dementia are safely monitored to avoid the discomfort and danger that
comes with being lost.
